Project 10 :- Brain Tumor Classification — PyTorch

About this project

This is my Tenth project in the domain of AI/ML/DL whose AI model is made by using the Pytorch and concepts of CNN(Convulational Neural Network) and trained on Brain-tumor dataset on Kaggle.

This Image Classification project detects whether there is an tumor or not in your given image of brain MRI.

This trained AI model detect the labels in image and gives you a Number(label) that it could find in your given image and also give a Confidence score that represents that how much confident model really is to give you that output.

Labels :-

  • Tumor
  • Notumor

So if you upload a image that belongs to any of these label's category then you will get a predicted class from model in this scenario notumor and tumor label and confidence score that shows the surety of predicted class.

Conclusion

I trained this model on google colab notebook's free GPU T4(it comes with a specfic duration of usage) for 10 epochs and then the minimum loss was about 0.02 something and i got 99% accuracy on testing dataset of Brain-tumor MRI classification dataset on Kaggle.

Test dataset metrics :-

  • 1).Accuracy :- 0.998001998001998
  • 2).F1 :- 0.9980000799168864
  • 3).Recall :- 0.9980657640232109

So final conclusion is that this model can predict the labels of images that are belongs to the category of labels of Brain-tumor MRI classification dataset images and sometime it can predict on also other images but image should be of Brain-tumor MRI.
